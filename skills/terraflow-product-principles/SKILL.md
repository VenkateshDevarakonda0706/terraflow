# TerraFlow Product Principles

## Purpose

Keep TerraFlow aligned with its identity as the Memory Layer of Earth.

## Activation Conditions

Use for product proposals, UI changes, UX flows, roadmap choices, homepage changes, and implementation decisions that affect how people experience TerraFlow.

## Rules

- Treat TerraFlow as the Memory Layer of Earth.
- Make the globe the homepage and keep it central to the experience.
- Put exploration before login.
- Avoid dashboard-style interfaces and SaaS-style layouts.
- Treat Earth as the product and discovery as the primary user action.
- Aim for premium, consumer-focused quality comparable to Apple, Airbnb, Arc Browser, and Google Earth.
- Never assume a feature works. Verify with evidence.
- Prefer root-cause fixes, low complexity, stability, and the core TerraFlow vision.

## Checklist

- Is the globe central?
- Does the experience feel magical?
- Is the interface exploration-first?
- Does the feature strengthen the core vision?
- Is login delayed until it is genuinely needed?
- Does the design avoid dashboard and SaaS conventions?
- Is the recommendation supported by evidence?
